Getting to know us

We provide integrated workplace solutions for clients under commercial, industrial and hospitality segments.

Our team of seasoned professionals is dedicated to delivering exceptional results, whether you are looking to lease, buy, or sell commercial properties. We offer a comprehensive range of services.

At Seven Dale Consultants, we pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence, integrity, and client satisfaction. Our personalised approach ensures that every client receives the attention and expertise they deserve, guiding them through every step of the real estate process with precision and care.

Transaction Advisory

Transaction advisory services encompass providing strategic guidance and support to organisations in their real estate needs, such as workplace solutions, brokerage, acquisitions, dispositions, and lease negotiations. These services aim to help companies optimise their real estate portfolios and make informed decisions to achieve their business objectives.
